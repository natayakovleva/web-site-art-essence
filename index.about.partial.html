About Our Workshop

Unleash your creativity with our pottery workshops.

Immerse yourself in the art of pottery with our workshop. This hands-on experience will take you on a journey of clay transformation using the mesmerizing pottery wheel. Throughout the workshop, our expert instructors will guide you through essential techniques, allowing you to unleash your creativity.

Clay Centering

Participants will learn the essential technique of centering the clay on the pottery wheel, which is crucial.

Vessel Shaping

Explore the art of shaping clay on the wheel to create various vessels such as bowls, cups, plates, and more.

Vessel Firing

Firing your clay vessel is the ultimate moment of anticipation during a pottery workshop.

Surface Decoration

Discover a variety of surface decoration techniques, including carving, stamping, and adding texture.

Duration: The workshop takes three hours, providing ample time for participants to delve into the process of pottery making on the wheel.

Difficulty Level: This workshop is designed for beginners and does not require prior experience with pottery or the pottery wheel. Our skilled instructors will guide participants through the fundamental techniques step by step.

Materials: All necessary materials will be provided, including clay specifically formulated for wheel throwing, pottery wheels, and a range of shaping tools.

During the workshop, you will learn the intricate process of clay centering, where you'll discover the delicate balance between pressure, speed, and precision. Mastering this technique is the key to creating symmetrical and well-proportioned pottery pieces.

As you progress, you will dive into vessel shaping, where you'll witness the clay come to life as it takes the form of bowls, cups, plates, and other creations.
